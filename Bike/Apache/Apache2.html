Bike Detail

Description:

For performance enthusiasts seeking speed, style, and precise handling, the TVS Apache is a well-liked line of fast motorbikes. Each model in the Apache line, which was created by TVS Motor Company, offers cutting-edge features and aggressive style.Apache bikes are well-known for their strong engines, agile braking systems, and crisp aerodynamics. They provide an exhilarating riding experience on highways and in urban traffic. Race-inspired features like Glide Through Traffic (GTT), Race Tuned Fuel Injection (RT-Fi), and SmartXonnect digital displays make the RTR series especially popular. Young riders and racing aficionados seeking a combination between performance, technology, and price may find the Apache appealing.

Features:

  • Engine Capacity (cc): 159.7cc
  • Mileage: 45-50 km/l
  • Fuel Consumption: ~0.020-0.022 liters/km (approximately)
